# Reflection

## Learning points

The team has learned:

* to document and work with transition process to the customer;
* to visualize data app statistics.

## Validated assumptions

* Statistics looks understandable;
* Soft deletion is validated;
* New access restrictions confirmed;
* Transcription patching is intuitive.

## Friction and gaps

* Timer for soft-deletion should be extended to 60 seconds instead of 30;
* Document how to train ASR model.

## Planned response

Two PBIs were created in correspondence to friction and gaps:

* [Extend soft deletion timer](https://github.com/SWP-Team20/Bilingual-speech-recognition/issues/302),
* [Document ASR training process](https://github.com/SWP-Team20/Bilingual-speech-recognition/issues/305).

Moreover, two other PBIs are planned to the next sprint, such as:

* [US-017: Light and dark themes](https://github.com/SWP-Team20/Bilingual-speech-recognition/issues/21),
* [US-029: Speaker's words](https://github.com/SWP-Team20/Bilingual-speech-recognition/issues/257).
