# Sprint Retrospective

## What went well

- All user stories were completed within their allocated story points
- User stories were chosen based on customer feedback, so the work completed was fully aligned with the goals set

## What did not go well

- Some tasks weren't finished on time by the people responsible for them, so other team members had to step in and help complete them

## What we changed compared to the previous Sprint based on the previous Sprint Retrospective

- Improved our task estimation to set more realistic deadlines during sprint planning
- Increased team efficiency by ensuring everyone was informed about upcoming organizational tasks

## Concrete process improvements for the next Sprint

- Set up a mid-Sprint check to spot delayed tasks early and redistribute the load if needed
Make sure everyone realistically estimates their capacity during Sprint planning to avoid shifting work later
