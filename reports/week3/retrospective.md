
#Sprint Retrospective

## What went well: 

- All user stories were completed within their allocated story points
- The selected user stories proved to be highly relevant to the customer
- Every team member clearly understood their responsibilities and delivered high-quality work

## What did not go well:

- Lack of full visibility into organizational tasks across all team members
- Some initial task estimations were inaccurate due to unexpected technical complexities
- Some communication gaps occurred during the Sprint, affecting effectiveness

## Action points:

- Improve Sprint planning and estimation techniques to ensure realistic commitments
- Increase team engagement across all organizational and agile processes
