# Customer Meeting Summary

## Overview

The meeting was conducted with researchers involved in a long-term bilingual (Russian–Tatar) family language study.

---

## Key Requirements

- Automatic transcription of long audio recordings
- Speaker identification (mother, father, child)
- Corpus management interface for researchers
- Ability to edit transcripts manually
- Support for multilingual data (Russian and Tatar)
- Search and filtering capabilities

---

## Key Feedback

- Manual transcription is too time-consuming and must be automated
- Speaker separation is more important than perfect transcription accuracy
- The system should prioritize usability for non-technical researchers
- Child speech is difficult and not required for initial version
- Tatar language support is desirable but technically challenging

---

## Decisions

- Focus MVP on transcription + corpus management
- Use existing ASR and diarisation tools
- Defer advanced language processing features

---

## Outcome

The project scope was confirmed and refined. The team will focus on building a minimal but functional research tool for managing bilingual audio corpora.
